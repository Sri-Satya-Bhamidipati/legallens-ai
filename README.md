# ⚖️ LegalLens AI

LegalLens AI is an AI-powered web application that helps users understand legal documents before signing them.

It transforms complex contracts into **clear, structured insights** with risk detection, decision support, and actionable suggestions.

---

## 🚨 Problem

Most people sign legal documents without fully understanding the risks involved.

This leads to:
- Financial loss
- Unfair obligations
- Long-term legal consequences

---

## 💡 Solution

LegalLens AI analyzes legal documents and provides:

- 📊 Risk detection (High / Moderate)
- 📌 Clause breakdown
- 🧠 Decision support (Safe / Review / High Risk)
- 💡 Suggestions to improve contract terms
- ⚖️ Legal context (India)
- 🔍 Highlighted explanations for risky clauses

---

## ✨ Key Features

- AI-powered contract analysis
- Decision-focused output (not just summaries)
- Risk classification system
- “What you could lose” insights
- Negotiation suggestions
- Opposite party perspective
- Clean, structured dashboard UI
- Try Sample Document (demo-ready feature)
